About: The past two summers, I worked as a Student Park Ranger at Ontario Parks. Throughout my time, I worked as a Gate Attendant, Assistant Discovery Guide, and Maintenance Student. Despite these changes, I was constantly having fun outdoors and enjoying meeting all the campers who stopped by. Each year, the Cochrane Cluster Parks would create Staff Sweaters. Below are the deisgns I drew.

2022

2022 final design

About: Ivanhoe Lake Provincial Park is famous for being located near an area with albino moose. Thus, my coworkers and I thought it would be nice to include a moose in the design. Moreover, that year, many of us spent time swimming in the lake and our favourite floatie was a duck ring.

About: During the summer of 2021, a mother fox made a den on Ivanhoe's main road and we constantly saw her. She became a mascot of sorts that summer. Thus, we thought it would be fitting to include her in the sweater deisgn.
