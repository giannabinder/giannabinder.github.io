Hello!

I am Gianna, an Electrical Engineering student at the University of Waterloo, passionate about using technology to promote sustainability and animal welfare in agriculture. As the global population grows, the need for increased food production becomes more urgent. I believe smart farming techniques, such as automation and precision agriculture, can optimize food production while preserving resources and reducing environmental impact. I have experience in customer service, maintenance and using power tools, working with children, media promotion, and event planning. Currently, I am expanding my skillset by learning AutoCad and microcontrollers and honing my programming abilities to become a leading innovator in green technology.
