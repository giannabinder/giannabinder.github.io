Bonjour!

About: I have always been drawn to the French language. I started learning it at the age of five and continued through high school. My dedication and hard work paid off as I was awarded the French Award in my grade 10 and 12 year. I was so passionate about the language that I even began teaching French in grade 11. The French language has a certain elegance and sophistication that I find captivating and extremely fun to use. Additionally, French is a widely spoken language and being able to speak it opens up many professional and personal opportunities. It is a language that I will continue to study and use throughout my life.
